2021 Annual Location Fees Will Be Drafted on January 21, 2021

2021 ARC travel agency location fees will be drafted from designated bank accounts on January 21, 2020. Any bank account changes or voluntary deletions will need to be completed by January 7, 2021. Additional 2021 fee information can be found here.

Reminder: Settlement Schedule Change In Effect

ARC’s draft and disbursement day has changed from Friday, five days after the sales week closes, to the following Wednesday, 10 days after the sales week closes. This change went into effect for PED 03/29/20 and will repeat weekly until further notice. A visual representation of these changes can be found here, and a list of FAQs is available here.
