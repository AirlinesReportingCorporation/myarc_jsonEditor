Void / Modification Window and Sales Report Submission Extended Due to Tropical Storm Dorian - Florida, Georgia and Puerto Rico

Due to Tropical Storm Dorian, ARC will be extending the void and modification window for all tickets issued on Tuesday, August 27, through Wednesday, August 28, for all agency locations (home offices and branches) in Florida, Georgia and Puerto Rico. The window will remain open until 11:59 p.m. EDT Friday, August 30. The sales report submission deadline for the last report with Period Ending Date (PED) ending Sunday, September 1, is also being extended to 11:59 p.m. EDT Friday, September 6.

Please be safe and know that ARC is available to support your agency during this difficult time.

If you have questions, please contact ARC’s Disaster Relief Center Toll Free at 800.816.8147, or email us at ccchelp@arccorp.com.
