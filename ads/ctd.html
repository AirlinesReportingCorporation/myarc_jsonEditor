ARC ANNOUCEMENT

Void / Modification Window and Sales Report Submission Extended Due to Tropical Storm Barry

Due to Tropical Storm Barry, ARC will be extending the void and modification window for all tickets issued on Friday, July 12, through Monday, July 15, for all agency locations (home offices and branches) in the state of Louisiana. The window will remain open until 11:59 p.m. EDT Thursday, July 18.

The sales report submission deadline for the last report with Period Ending Date (PED) ending Sunday, July 14, is also being extended to 11:59 p.m. EDT Thursday, July 18.

Please be safe and know that ARC is available to support your agency during this difficult time. If you have questions, please contact ARC’s Disaster Relief Center toll free at 1-800-816-8147, or email us at ccchelp@arccorp.com.
