ARC Discontinues Automated Ticket and Boarding Pass

Effective December 17, 2020, ARC is discontinuing its print product line of Automated Ticket and Boarding Pass (ATB) stock and Mini-Itin stock. ATB and Mini-Itin stock may be purchased through that date and used until a travel agency's supplies are exhausted. Orders can be made via the Document Ordering System in My ARC. Changes to ARCâ€™s Weekly Cash Disbursement and Refund Processes Effective PED 11/08/2020

ARC has been informed by its settlement processing bank, U.S. Bank, that all ACH debits must be secured by the bank for three business days prior to being disbursed as part of the ACH process. To comply with these bank requirements, ARC will require all airlines to wire any funds owed and process cash debits from its travel agency customers on the Friday following the end of the sales week (PED+5). ARC will then process wires to its airline and credits to its travel agency customers the following Wednesday (PED+10).
