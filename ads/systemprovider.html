In 2020, many frequent travelers found themselves grounded far longer than they ever expected, due to the coronavirus pandemic. When travelers increasingly take to the skies again, how can they approach their travels more responsibly and sustainably?

In this episode, Bruce Poon Tip, founder of G Adventures and author of Looptail: How One Company Changed the World by Reinventing Business and UNLEARN: The Year the Earth Stood Still, discusses his reflections on 2020 and how the travel industry can bounce back with a greater focus on sustainability and responsible travel.
