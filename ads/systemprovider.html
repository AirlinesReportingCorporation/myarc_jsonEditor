ARC Discontinues Automated Ticket and Boarding Pass

Effective December 17, 2020, ARC is discontinuing its print product line of Automated Ticket and Boarding Pass (ATB) stock and Mini-Itin stock. ATB and Mini-Itin stock may be purchased through that date and used until a travel agency's supplies are exhausted. Orders can be made via the Document Ordering System in My ARC.
