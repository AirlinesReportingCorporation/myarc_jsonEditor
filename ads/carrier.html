ARC Discontinues Automated Ticket and Boarding Pass

Effective December 17, 2020, ARC is discontinuing its print product line of Automated Ticket and Boarding Pass (ATB) stock and Mini-Itin stock. ATB and Mini-Itin stock may be purchased through that date and used until a travel agency's supplies are exhausted. Orders can be made via the Document Ordering System in My ARC. Reminder: Settlement Schedule Change In Effect

ARC’s cash draft and disbursement day has changed from Friday, five days after the sales week closes, to the following Wednesday, 10 days after the sales week closes. Additionally, the processing of all cash sales refunds have been deferred to the last processing/output cycle of the week (i.e., Cycle 7). These changes went into effect for PED 03/29/20 and will repeat weekly until further notice. Click here for more information.
