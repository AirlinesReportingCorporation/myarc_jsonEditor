Settlement Schedule Change Effective PED 03/29/20

ARC is moving its cash draft and disbursement day from Friday to the following Wednesday, ten days after the sales week closes, beginning PED 03/29/20 (with disbursement moving from Friday, April 3, to Wednesday, April 8) and will repeat weekly until further notice. The Tuesday sales report submission deadline will not change, and credit card transactions are not affected. For more information, click here for FAQs for travel agencies.
