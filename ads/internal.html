Void / Modification Window and Sales Report Submission Extended Due to Hurricane Dorian - Florida and Georgia

Due to Hurricane Dorian, ARC will be extending the void and modification window for all tickets issued on Tuesday, August 27 through Sunday Sep 1, for all agency locations (home offices and branches) in Florida and Georgia. The window will remain open until 11:59 EDT Friday Sep 6. The sales reporting submission deadline for PED 01SEP is also extended to 11:59PM EDT, Friday September 6.

ARC will continue to monitor Hurricane Dorian and advice of extensions for sales reporting PED 08SEP.

Please be safe and know that ARC is available to support your agency during this difficult time.

If you have questions, please contact ARC’s Disaster Relief Center Toll Free at 800.816.8147, or email us at ccchelp@arccorp.com.
