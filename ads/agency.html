ARC ANNOUCEMENT

Notice of Changes to the ARA: Effective February 1, 2019

Following ARC’s consultation with travel agency representatives, ARC’s Board of Directors approved a number of changes to the Agent Reporting Agreement (ARA). The primary ARA changes relate to updates to the types of agency contacts (including email addresses), changes to the ARC Specialist requirement, elimination of the Satellite Ticket Printer (STP) location classification, and the elimination of references to obsolete ARC traffic documents. A summary of these changes can be found here for your reference. To review the ARA changes in more detail, click here.

These changes will take effect on February 1, 2019. Please note that in accordance with Section 5 of the ARA, your continued participation with ARC after February 1, 2019, will serve as your acceptance of the amendments.
